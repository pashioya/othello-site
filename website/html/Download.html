How to download Othello

  • Download the userIntegrityCheck
  • Unzip userIntegrityCheck and put the game files(zipped or unzipped) you want to check in the userIntegrityCheck folder
  • open (windows)cmd or Terminal(Mac)
  • Enter the command "bash Downloads/userIntegrityCheck/userIntegrityScript.sh"
  • The output should tell you whether or not your file is up to date
